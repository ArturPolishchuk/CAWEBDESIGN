Playful Lambs

Brookfield Farm lamb is delicious, tender and great value. Our lamb is raised in a small organically certified flock and carefully reared. When ready for eating, the lamb has been brought to a local, fully accredited, craft butcher, carefully handled to avoid stress and processed with a few of its flockmates. Tired Lambs

What you are getting is a completely different animal from the anonymous conveyor belt lamb you normally buy from the supermarket. It is also great value. The lambs are organically certified and eat organic grass and a little ration in winter. Buying lamb direct from Brookfield Farm not only saves money, but you will notice the difference in taste and quality in our lambs that are produced locally, fresh from our farm, and delivered directly to your door.
